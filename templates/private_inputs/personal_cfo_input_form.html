Safety Rules

  • Do not enter account numbers, raw account IDs, NRIC, FIN, passwords, API keys, tokens, tax reference numbers, or raw addresses.
  • Use labels and stable local hashes such as property_id_hash, loan_id_hash, and manual account labels.
  • Save real values only in ignored local files under private_inputs/.
